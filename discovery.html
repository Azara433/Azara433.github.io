Arrowpoint Nature Resort

Arrowpoint is a prime eco-tourism location in Guyana. Visitors are housed in thatched roof cabins, each with electricity and ensuite bathrooms. Arrowpoint is a great location for those who want to enjoy the nature of Guyana - many species of birds can be spotted there, and most tour options also include hiking and nature walks. The resort is also located near a creek, so visitors can enjoy swimming and kayaking. Tours and accommodations here can be booked through the Roraima Airways website, or their Facebook page.
